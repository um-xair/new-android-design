FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How can I track my order?

You can track your order by visiting the "Order History" section in your account. You'll find real-time updates on your order status.

Q2: What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Please ensure the product is in its original packaging and unused. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

Q3: How do I change my account details?

To change your account details, go to the "Profile" section in your account. From there, you can update your email, phone number, and other personal information.

Q4: How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via email at support@quinton.com.my or by phone at +60 12-345 6789. We're available from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
